Three of PBS's pocketbook-size draft notebook form the nucleus od The Henry E. Huntington Library's collection of Shelley manuscripts. These manuscript notebooks, formerley known as the Bixby Notebooks, are shelfmarked HM 2111, HM 2176, and HM 2177. Huntington Shelley Notebook HM 2177 contains drafts of several poems, two prefaces, many poetic fragments, and prose notes written by PBS for the most part between spring 1918 and spring 1820. With its diverse contents, this manuscript presents a detailed picture of PBS as working writer and student of literature during his annus mirabilis.

For the time being, we present here only ff. 45v (rev.) – 46v (rev.), which contain a draft of “My lost William” (“To William Shelley”).
